# YOUR TASK

Your task is to analyze a list of clinical trials for a specific patient and decide which ones should be used to create a final therapy recommendation.

**CRITICAL:** You are evaluating trials as **EVIDENCE SOURCES** for treatment decisions, NOT as enrollment opportunities. Trials with published results (completed, active but not recruiting) provide valuable clinical evidence.

For each trial, you must decide whether to **INCLUDE** it or **EXCLUDE** it, and provide a clear reason why. This analysis will explain why some trials are chosen for the final recommendation and others are not.

---

# REASONING FRAMEWORK

Before making INCLUDE/EXCLUDE decisions, apply this reasoning for each trial:

1. **Evidence Availability:** Does the trial have published results (peer-reviewed publications, congress abstracts, posted results)?
2. **Clinical Relevance:** Does the evidence address the patient's condition and treatment needs?
3. **Evidence Quality:** What is the study phase and design (Phase 3 > Phase 2 > Phase 1)?

**NOTE:** Recruitment status (recruiting, not recruiting, completed) is NOT a criterion for exclusion. Focus on whether the trial provides actionable clinical evidence.
